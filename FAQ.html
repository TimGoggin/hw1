Pricing

Q: Can I buy this product?
A: No. This site was designed and published as part of the COMP 333 Software Engineering class at Wesleyan University. The described platform is not publicly available but rather is created as a training exercise.
Q: How does your product help artists?
A: Artists can gain exposure for their songs by putting them out there to be reviewed! Artists keep all rights and profits associated with the music.

Q: Why are so many features behind a paywall?
A: We are committed to creating a good user experience - so we keep the number of ads to a minimum. This means that we must charge in order to provide servers to upload songs.
Q: Will there be an annual payment option?
